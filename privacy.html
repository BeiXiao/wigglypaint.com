Privacy Policy

WigglyPaint - Free Online Drawing Tool

TL;DR: WigglyPaint respects your privacy. We don't collect personal data, don't use cookies for tracking, and all your drawings are processed locally on your device. Your art stays private unless you choose to share it.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WigglyPaint. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website wigglypaint.com or use our mobile application.

WigglyPaint is a free online drawing tool that creates animated wiggly lines. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

2. Information We Don't Collect

WigglyPaint is designed with privacy in mind. We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account registration data (no accounts required)
  • Payment information (the service is completely free)
  • Your drawings or artwork (all processing is local)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or fingerprinting data
  • Social media profiles or connections

3. Information We May Collect

We may collect minimal, non-personal information for basic functionality:

3.1 Technical Information

  • Browser type and version - To ensure compatibility
  • Operating system - To optimize performance
  • Screen resolution - To provide the best user experience
  • General location (country/region) - For language preferences only

3.2 Usage Analytics

  • Number of visitors (aggregated, anonymous)
  • Popular features and tools usage
  • Performance metrics (loading times, errors)
  • Referral sources (which websites link to us)

Note: All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users.

4. How Your Art is Processed

Local Processing: All your drawings, animations, and creative work are processed entirely on your device. Your artwork never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to download or share it.

4.1 Drawing Data

  • All drawing operations happen in your browser's memory
  • No drawings are uploaded to our servers
  • When you export a GIF, it's created locally on your device
  • We cannot see, access, or store your artwork

4.2 Temporary Storage

  • Drawings may be temporarily stored in your browser's local storage
  • This data remains on your device and is automatically cleared when you close the browser
  • You can manually clear this data through your browser settings

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 Essential Cookies

WigglyPaint uses minimal essential cookies to:

  • Remember your brush preferences during your session
  • Maintain tool settings while you draw
  • Ensure the application functions properly

5.2 No Tracking Cookies

We DO NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party analytics cookies (like Google Analytics)
  • Cross-site tracking mechanisms

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Content Delivery Network (CDN)

We may use CDN services to deliver our application faster. These services may log basic technical information (IP address, request time) but do not track individual users.

6.2 No Third-Party Tracking

We do not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Facebook Pixel or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection platforms

7. Data Security

7.1 Local Security

Since all your data is processed locally:

  • Your drawings are only as secure as your device
  • We recommend keeping your device secure and updated
  • Use trusted networks when accessing WigglyPaint

7.2 Website Security

  • Our website is served over HTTPS for secure communication
  • We regularly update our security measures
  • No sensitive data is transmitted to our servers

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WigglyPaint is safe for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No account registration is required
  • No personal data is collected from users of any age
  • Parents can safely allow children to use WigglyPaint

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users (GDPR Compliance)

9.1 European Union Users

For users in the EU, under GDPR:

  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest for providing the service
  • Data Minimization: We collect only essential technical data
  • Right to Access: Contact us for any data inquiries
  • Right to Deletion: Clear your browser data to remove local storage
  • Data Portability: Export your creations directly from the app

9.2 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we might have (which is minimal)
  • Request deletion of data
  • Object to data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

9.3 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents, under CCPA:

  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not share personal information for commercial purposes
  • You can request disclosure of data collection practices
  • You can request deletion of any personal information

Since WigglyPaint collects minimal non-personal data, most CCPA requests will result in confirmation that no personal data is collected.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will post the updated policy on this page
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we may add a notice on our homepage
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

Website: wigglypaint.com

Email: support@wigglypaint.com

Response Time

We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days. For urgent matters, please indicate "URGENT" in your subject line.

Data Subject Requests

For GDPR or CCPA requests, please include:

  • Your specific request (access, deletion, etc.)
  • Approximate dates of service usage
  • Any relevant details to help us assist you

12. Summary

Our Privacy Commitment

  • No personal data collection
  • No account registration required
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No tracking or advertising cookies
  • Your art stays private
  • COPPA and GDPR compliant
  • Completely free to use

Bottom line: WigglyPaint is designed to respect your privacy. Create, animate, and share your art with confidence!
